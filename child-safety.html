POPNOW Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Protecting Minors

POPNOW is committed to creating a safe environment for all users, especially minors. We have implemented comprehensive policies and systems to prevent child exploitation and abuse on our platform.

Age Requirements

  • Users must be 13 years or older to create a POPNOW account
  • Age verification is required during account creation
  • Accounts found to belong to users under 13 are immediately terminated

Content Moderation

  • All uploaded videos undergo automated and human moderation review
  • Content must be approved before becoming visible on the platform
  • Inappropriate content depicting or exploiting minors is immediately removed
  • Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) Prevention

Zero Tolerance Policy

POPNOW maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and child exploitation.

  • Automated content scanning using industry-standard detection technology
  • Immediate removal of any suspected CSAM content
  • Mandatory reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies worldwide
  • Permanent ban of accounts that violate this policy
  • IP address and device blocking to prevent repeat offenses

User Reporting System

  • In-app reporting feature available on all video content and user profiles
  • Dedicated "Child Safety Concerns" reporting category
  • 24/7 monitoring of reported content
  • All reports reviewed within 24 hours
  • Anonymous reporting supported

Parental Controls

  • Age-appropriate content filtering
  • Account privacy settings for users under 18
  • Restricted direct messaging for minor accounts

Compliance & Reporting

POPNOW complies with all relevant child safety laws including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Local and national child protection legislation

We report all suspected child exploitation to:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via CyberTipline
  • Local law enforcement authorities
  • Relevant international agencies as required by law

Contact for Child Safety Concerns

Email: popnowglobal@gmail.com

Report to NCMEC: www.cybertipline.org

Emergency: Contact your local law enforcement immediately

Our designated child safety team is available to discuss our CSAM prevention practices and compliance measures.
