Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your POPNOW account, please review the information below carefully before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion, the following will occur:

  • All your videos will be permanently deleted - This includes all videos you've uploaded to POPNOW
  • Your profile information will be removed - Username, email, profile picture, and bio will be deleted
  • POPCoins will be forfeited - All POPCoins in your account will be lost with no refund
  • Premium subscription will be cancelled - If you have an active Premium subscription, it will be cancelled (no refund for remaining time)
  • Transaction history may be retained - We may retain certain transaction records for legal compliance and fraud prevention

⚠️ WARNING: Account deletion is PERMANENT and CANNOT be undone. All your data will be lost forever.

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Take a break: You can simply stop using the app without deleting your account
  • Adjust privacy settings: Change your location privacy settings if that's your concern
  • Cancel Premium: If cost is the issue, you can cancel Premium and continue using the free version

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your POPNOW account, please contact us at:

support@popnow.world

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Required Information

Please include the following in your email:

  • Your POPNOW username
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is permanent

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Videos: Deleted immediately
  • Profile data: Deleted within 7 days
  • Transaction records: May be retained for up to 7 years for legal compliance
  • Aggregated analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

Subscription Cancellation

If you have an active Premium subscription, you must also cancel it through your device settings:

iOS:

  • Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → POPNOW → Cancel Subscription

Android:

  • Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → POPNOW → Cancel Subscription

Deleting your account does NOT automatically cancel your subscription. You are responsible for cancelling it separately.

Need Help?

If you're experiencing issues with the app or have questions, please contact us at support@popnow.world before deleting your account. We're here to help!
